How do I get started with SynkUp?
1. Download the app from the App Store or Google Play
2. Create an account with your email or sign in with Google
3. Connect your calendar
4. Start scheduling smarter meetings!
Is my data secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. We never sell your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
How does the AI scheduling work?
Our AI analyzes everyone's availability, preferences, and past meeting patterns to suggest optimal times and venues that work for all participants.
Can I use SynkUp offline?
Yes! You can view your scheduled meetings and make plans offline. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from the Settings menu in the app. This will permanently remove all your data. If you need assistance, contact us at support@synkup.app.
What calendars does SynkUp support?
SynkUp integrates with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Outlook. More calendar integrations are coming soon!
Is SynkUp free?
SynkUp is currently free to use with all core features. Premium features may be introduced in the future.
